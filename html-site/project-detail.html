PROJECT OVERVIEW ——

Riverside Heights is a premier residential community nestled in the heart of Orange County, California. Designed for discerning buyers seeking refined suburban living, the development combines estate-caliber architecture with generous lot sizes and curated landscaping throughout.

Each residence is thoughtfully sited to maximize privacy, natural light, and views of the surrounding hillside terrain. The master plan integrates tree-lined streetscapes, community parks, and seamless access to Orange County's key commercial and civic corridors.

With individual title deeds and flexible lot configurations, Riverside Heights represents an exceptional opportunity for primary residence, generational investment, or custom home construction in one of Southern California's most sought-after growth markets.

→ Minutes from John Wayne Airport and the I-5 / SR-55 interchange
→ Curated streetscape with drought-tolerant native landscaping
→ Underground utilities and smart-home infrastructure pre-wired per lot
→ Proximity to top-rated Irvine Unified and Orange County school districts
